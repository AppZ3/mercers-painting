## 2. Local-business backlinks (higher quality, harder)

These are the backlinks that move the needle. Each requires real outreach, but a single .au business or community-org link is worth 50 directory listings.

### A. Ballina Chamber of Commerce + Byron Bay Chamber of Commerce

**Target URLs:**
- ballinachamber.com.au
- byronchamberofcommerce.com.au

Most chambers list member businesses on their site with a do-follow link. Annual membership is usually $200-400 and includes the listing plus networking events. The backlink alone is worth it for SEO; the local relationships are the bonus.

**Email template:**

```
Subject: New member enquiry — Mercer's Precision Painting (Lennox Head)

Hi [Chamber team],

I'm Stanton Mercer, owner of Mercer's Precision Painting in Lennox Head. We've been painting across the Northern Rivers since 1999 — residential, commercial, and a fair bit of work for local hospitality and retail.

I'd like to enquire about joining the [Ballina / Byron] Chamber of Commerce. Could you send me through the membership levels and what's included?

Happy to come down for a chat or grab a coffee if easier.

Cheers,
Stanton Mercer
Mercer's Precision Painting
0427 088 838
mercersprecisionpainting.com
```

### B. Local commercial clients (existing relationships)

Mercer's has done commercial work across the Northern Rivers. Some of those businesses have websites with "suppliers" or "trusted trades" pages. A backlink from a real local business that Mercer's has worked for is worth gold.

**Action:** make a list of the 5-10 best commercial jobs from the last 2 years. For each, find the business website. Check if they have a suppliers/trades/partners page. If they do, request a link. If they don't, suggest they add one.

**Email template (warm outreach to existing client):**

```
Subject: Quick favour — link from your site?

Hi [Name],

Hope you're well. I'm doing some work on the website ([mercersprecisionpainting.com](https://mercersprecisionpainting.com)) to help local Northern Rivers customers find us more easily on Google.

One of the things that helps is getting a link from a real local business who's worked with us. Would you be open to adding Mercer's Precision Painting to a "suppliers" or "trusted trades" section on your site? Even a short mention with a link helps a lot.

Happy to send over a 50-word blurb you can paste in — no work for you.

Thanks for considering it,
Stanton
0427 088 838
```

**The 50-word blurb to send when they agree:**

> Mercer's Precision Painting handled our [interior/exterior/commercial] painting. Stanton and the team have been doing quality work across the Northern Rivers since 1999. Recommended.

### D. Local tradies network (link exchange / referral)

Build relationships with non-competing trades for cross-referrals. Often they'll add a "trusted trades" or "we recommend" page on their site.

**Targets:**
- Local builders (especially custom home builders in Lennox/Byron/Ballina)
- Plasterers and renderers (their work usually needs painting after)
- Real estate agents (pre-sale repaint referrals)
- Interior designers (colour and finish referrals)

**Approach:** start with a coffee. Ask for a referral arrangement. Mention the website link as part of the relationship. Don't lead with "give me a backlink."
