Commercial work we take on

Commercial painting is a different discipline from residential. Trading hours, public liability, faster cure times, brand colour-matching, site supervision. We've delivered all of it across the Northern Rivers for two and a half decades.

Shopfronts & retail

Interior and exterior shop repaints, brand colour-matching, signage prep. We work around trading hours including after-hours and overnight when a daytime job would lose you customers.

Offices & professional spaces

Open-plan, reception areas, boardrooms, fit-out refreshes. Low-odour paints, weekend scheduling, dust containment so staff can return Monday to a clean space.

Hospitality & fit-outs

Cafes, pubs, restaurants. We've painted plenty of Northern Rivers hospo, from boutique cafes in Bangalow to surf-side spaces in Lennox. Timeline-critical work delivered on time.

Body corporate & strata

Apartment blocks, complex common areas, exterior repaints. We coordinate with strata managers, provide insurance certificates and stage work to minimise resident disruption.

Industrial & warehouse

Warehouses, sheds, factories, line-marking-adjacent floors. Airless spray for large areas. Industrial-grade epoxy and chlorinated rubber finishes where the substrate demands.

Schools, aged care & healthcare

Low-VOC formulations, infection-control awareness, working in occupied spaces with sensitivity. WWCC and police checks available for staff on request.

Our commercial process

For commercial jobs we work to a proper project plan. You get clarity on cost, schedule and how we'll work around your operations. No surprises mid-job.

  • Site inspection. Free, scoped, scheduled around your operations. We measure properly and document existing conditions.
  • Written quote. Itemised by area and scope. Materials specified. Payment terms clear. Insurance certificates available.
  • Schedule agreement. Work staged to suit your trading hours. After-hours, overnight or weekend work scheduled in advance.
  • Site setup. Hoardings, signage, public liability protection where customers or staff are present. WHS documentation provided.
  • Execution. Daily progress reports. Brand colour-matching verified before bulk application. Touch-ups documented.
  • Sign-off & warranty. Final walkthrough with you or your facility manager. Written workmanship warranty provided.

Commercial painting FAQ

Do you work after hours so we can stay open during the day?
Yes. For retail and hospitality clients we regularly work evenings, overnight and weekends so daytime trading continues. Quoted accordingly upfront.
Can you provide insurance and OH&S documentation?
Yes. We carry public liability, workers compensation and product warranties. Insurance certificates of currency, SWMS and JSEA documentation available on request before work starts.
Do you work alongside other trades on a fit-out?
Often. We coordinate with builders, electricians, joiners, plasterers and flooring contractors on full fit-outs. Stanton runs the painting scope and coordinates with the head contractor.
What about body corporate work where decisions take time?
We understand the strata process. We can provide quotes at the right level of detail for committee approval, offer phased pricing for staged work and coordinate with the strata manager throughout.
Do you do industrial-grade coatings?
Yes. Epoxy floor coatings, chlorinated rubber for splash zones, two-pack polyurethanes and industrial primers for steel structures. Specified to the application.
