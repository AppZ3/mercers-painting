Our Story

A standard set by the family itself.

Stanton Mercer founded Mercer's Precision Painting in Lennox Head over fifteen years ago with a simple belief: that the quality of the work speaks louder than any advertisement. Behind every project, his wife Hayley keeps the business running — managing quotes, scheduling, client communication, and everything in between. Without her, none of it would work.

Stanton's two sons have grown up watching that same standard applied to every job, big or small. From heritage coastal homes to large-scale commercial and industrial projects, the approach never changes — thorough preparation, premium materials, and a finished result the whole family is proud to put their name on.

What We Do

Every surface, every finish.

From a single feature wall to a full industrial facility — our scope covers every substrate and environment that calls for a considered hand.

01

Residential

Precision interior and exterior work for homes across the Northern Rivers — new builds, renovations, and coastal heritage restorations.

  • Interior walls, ceilings & trims
  • Exterior & coastal weatherboard
  • Cabinet & joinery painting
  • Feature walls & colour consulting
  • Deck, fence & pergola staining
  • Texture & decorative finishes
02

Commercial

Professional, minimal-disruption painting for offices, retail, hospitality and strata — delivered on schedule and to spec.

  • Office & retail fit-outs
  • Hospitality & accommodation
  • Strata & body corporate
  • Specialty & feature finishes
  • Heritage colour & restoration
  • Anti-graffiti systems
03

Industrial

Heavy-duty protective coatings for structures, plant and equipment — marine-grade systems engineered for the Northern Rivers climate.

  • Epoxy floor coatings
  • Structural steel & pipework
  • Rust treatment & corrosion control
  • Marine-grade protective systems
  • Warehouses & sheds
  • Line marking
04

Roof Restoration

Full roof restoration and protective coating services for residential and commercial roofs — extending lifespan and improving weather resistance.

  • Roof cleaning & preparation
  • Ridge capping repointing
  • Membrane & protective coatings
  • Metal & Colorbond roof painting
  • Tile roof restoration
  • Leak sealing & waterproofing
05

Driveways

Durable driveway coatings and resurfacing for concrete and asphalt surfaces — UV-stable, slip-resistant, and built to last in the Northern Rivers climate.

  • Concrete sealing & coating
  • Epoxy driveway systems
  • Slip-resistant texture finishes
  • Crack repair & resurfacing
  • Colour & decorative finishes
  • UV-stable topcoat application
06

Timber Works

Specialist timber finishing and preservation for decks, cladding, fences and joinery — protecting the natural character of wood in coastal environments.

  • Deck oiling & staining
  • Timber cladding & weatherboard
  • Fence & pergola treatments
  • Interior timber & joinery
  • Sanding & surface preparation
  • Timber preservation systems

How We Work

A process built around your project.

Good painting is 70% preparation. We don't cut corners in the lead-up — which is why our finishes last and our clients keep calling us back.

01

Consultation

We visit your property, assess the scope, and listen. No obligation, no pressure — just an honest conversation about your project.

02

Preparation

Surface prep is everything. We sand, fill, prime, and repair — so the finish coat is applied to a perfect substrate.

03

Application

Premium materials, applied with care. We protect your space, work methodically, and keep the site clean throughout.

04

Handover

A final walkthrough together. We don't leave until you're satisfied — and our work is backed by a written warranty.
